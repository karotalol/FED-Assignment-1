John's Transformation: From Couch to 5K

Before: John, 35, struggled with low energy and a sedentary lifestyle, spending most of his time on the couch after work. He had never run a mile in his life.

After: Within six months of joining Fitlife Studio, John completed his first 5K run. Through personalized coaching and a mix of strength training and cardio sessions, John improved his stamina, lost 15 pounds, and gained newfound confidence.
